Informed Consent to Participate in Research

Claremont McKenna College

Project Title: Intertemporal Decisions

Principle Investigator: Cameron Shelton, McMahon Family Associate Professor of Political Economy

You are being invited to participate in a research study, which the Claremont McKenna College Institutional Review Board (IRB) has reviewed and approved for conduct by the investigators named here. This form is designed to provide you - as a human subject - with information about this study. If you have any questions or complaints about the informed consent process of this research study or your rights as a subject, please contact the IRB at the Claremont McKenna College Office of Institutional Research at (909) 607-8395 or IRB@cmc.edu. Also see www.cmc.edu/institutional-review-board for more information on research involving human subjects.


What is the purpose of the study?

We are attempting to measure how people choose between receiving a smaller reward at an early date and receiving a larger reward at a later date.

Eligibility

To take this survey you must be:

  • At least 18 years old
  • Financially independent

What will I do?

You will answer {{total_questions}} simple questions about your household economics and your personal politics. We expect these will be simple to answer.

After this, you will be taken to a screen to play a game. Essentially, the game will ask you to allocate tokens between two dates. If all the tokens are allocated to the later date, the value is $10. Tokens allocated to the early date are worth less than those allocated to the later date. You will be asked this question 45 times, for different combinations of early and late dates and different penalties for the early date.

Risks

We ask you some questions (income, political affiliation) whose answers you may wish to keep private. These answers will be stored anonymously. We will never link these answers to your email address.

Anytime you share information online there are risks. We are using a secure system to collect this data. We will store all information in secure servers under password. Data are anonymous. Once payment has been made, your email address will be discarded and only a study ID will be used.

Compensation

Payment is earned upon completion of the survey. We ask for your email address in order to pay you. Payment will be delivered via Amazon gift cards to this email address. The amounts and dates will be determined by your answers as mentioned above. Of the 45 scenarios you consider, we will draw one at random and pay you according to your choices. To this, we will add $5 to the early date and $5 to the late date. Thus, you will receive payment at two future dates. The total will be between $15 and $20.
We will never use your email address for anything other than sending you payment.

Questions about the research, complaints, or problems: Contact Cameron Shelton, cshelton@cmc.edu
